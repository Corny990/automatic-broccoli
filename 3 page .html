How to Carve a Pumpkin

Instructions

What you'll need:

Equipment/Tools

  • Scoop Tool
  • Filet Knife
  • Paring Knife

Materials

  • Disposable Tablecloth
  • Large Bowl
  • Sharpie

Step 1

Prep the carving area

Step 2

Gather your pumpkin carving tools

  • Scoop tool
  • Filet knife
  • Paring knife

Step 3

Cut a hole in the pumpkin

  • Cut around the stem of the pumpkin

Step 4

Scoop out the guts

  • Use your scooper to scrap out the guts and seeds of the pumpkin

Step 5

Draw your design on the pumpkin

  • Use your sharpie to draw out your design on your pumpkin

Step 6

Start cutting

    Use your knife and follow your line you drew on your pumpking to start carving
